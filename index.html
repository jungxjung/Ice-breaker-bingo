🧊⛏️Icebreaker Bingo

How to play?

Step 1: Pick a bingo card

Click 🔄 to get a new set of missions on your card. Pick one and stick to it untill the end!

Step 2: Breakout room

The moderator (RR) will send you to a breakout room. Each breakout room has 3 - 4 participants.

Step 3: Mingle

You have 3 minutes to say hi and find people who match the traits on your bingo card in this breakout room.

Step 4: Mark your card

Once you find the person with the correct trait, click on the box and input the person's name.

Step 5: Repeat step 3&4

We will do the same thing for at least 3 rounds. Don't forget to yell BINGO when you fill five boxes across, down or diagonal.
